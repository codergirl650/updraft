Our Journey

2024 - Present

UpDraft Engineering Launches

Founded by passionate high school students to make engineering education accessible to elementary and middle school students in the Austin area. Started with a vision to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem solvers.

Fall 2024

First Workshop Series

Successfully delivered our inaugural 8-week engineering workshop series, reaching 25+ students with hands-on projects including solar ovens, robotics, and coding fundamentals. Students built over 50 projects and learned from industry professionals.

Winter 2024

Community Partnerships Established

Formed partnerships with local schools, libraries, and community centers to expand our reach. Collaborated with professional engineers who volunteered as guest speakers and mentors, bringing real-world experience into our classrooms.

Spring 2025

Second Cohort Success

Expanded to serve 25+ more students with improved curriculum and additional modules. Introduced choice-based projects allowing students to pursue their specific interests in engineering, from biomedical devices to sustainable energy solutions.

2025 - Future

Growing Our Impact

Planning to expand our programs to reach more students across Austin. Developing new curriculum modules, building partnerships with STEM organizations, and working towards establishing UpDraft Engineering as a registered nonprofit to serve our community even more effectively.

Key Achievements

🎓

Student Success

50+ students have completed our engineering workshops, with 95% reporting increased interest in STEM careers. Many students continue to pursue engineering projects independently after completing the program.

🤝

Volunteer Engagement

Recruited and trained 10+ dedicated volunteer mentors and guest speakers from various engineering disciplines. Our volunteers have contributed over 200+ hours of their time to inspire young minds.

🔬

Hands-On Learning

Facilitated the creation of 100+ student projects including solar ovens, robotic vehicles, biomedical hand prototypes, and custom software applications. Every student gets to build, test, and present their work.

🌟

Free Access for All

Maintained our commitment to 100% free programming, providing all materials and equipment at no cost to families. We've also loaned computers to students who need them, removing barriers to participation.

💡

Innovative Curriculum

Developed a comprehensive engineering curriculum that balances theory with practice, covering mechanical, electrical, software, and biomedical engineering. Our modules are continuously refined based on student feedback.

🏆

Student Showcase Events

Hosted multiple project presentation events where students demonstrate their work to families and community members. These celebrations build confidence and public speaking skills while recognizing student achievements.
