Our Journey

2025 - Present

UpDraft Engineering Launches


Founded by passionate high school students to make engineering education accessible to elementary and middle school students in the Austin area. Started with a vision to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem solvers.

Fall 2025 - Present

Mentorships with Industry Professionals and Established


Collaborated with professional engineers who volunteered as guest speakers and mentors, bringing real-world experience into our classrooms.

2025 - Future

Growing Our Impact


Planning to expand our programs to reach more students across Austin. Developing new curriculum modules, building partnerships with STEM organizations, and working towards establishing UpDraft Engineering as a registered nonprofit to serve our community even more effectively. Developing more partnerships and expanding nationwide.

Key Achievements

🤝

Volunteer Engagement


Recruited and trained 5+ dedicated volunteer mentors and guest speakers from various engineering disciplines. Our volunteers, combined, have contributed over 200+ hours of their time to help inspire young minds.

🔬

Hands-On Learning


Facilitated the creation of many student projects including solar ovens, robotic vehicles, biomedical hand prototypes, and custom software applications. Every student gets to build, test, and present their work.

🌟

Free Access for All


Maintained our commitment to 100% free programming, providing all materials and equipment at no cost to families. We've also loaned computers to students who need them, removing barriers to participation.

💡

Innovative Curriculum

Developed a comprehensive engineering curriculum that balances theory with practice, covering mechanical, electrical, software, and biomedical engineering. Our modules are continuously refined based on feedback.
