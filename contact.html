Become a Student

Are you ready to explore engineering and build amazing projects? Our engineering series is completely free and open to all students in elementary and middle school. We provide all materials, including computers for students who need them.

Requirements:

  • Elementary or middle schooler
  • Curious about engineering and technology
  • Able to commit to the program
  • No prior experience necessary!

Volunteer as a Mentor

Share your engineering expertise and inspire young minds. We're looking for engineers, passionate high school STEM students, or STEM professionals to serve as mentors, guest speakers, or workshop leaders.

Opportunities:

  • Weekly mentorship (2 hours/week for 8 sessions)
  • Guest speaking engagements
  • Lead specialized workshops
  • Project support and troubleshooting

Make a Donation

Your financial support helps us provide free engineering education to more students. Every donation directly funds materials, equipment, and program expansion.

Your Impact:

  • $50 - Provides materials for one student's robotics project
  • $200 - Supplies a laptop for a student in need
  • $500 - Sponsors an entire student for the program
  • $2,000 - Funds a complete session
