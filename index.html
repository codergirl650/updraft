Our Mission

We believe every young person deserves access to quality engineering education. UpDraft Engineering provides free hands-on learning experiences that inspire curiosity, develop critical thinking, and prepare students for careers in engineering and technology. We hope to foster a collaborative environment where students can make friends, be themselves, and have fun learning engineering!
