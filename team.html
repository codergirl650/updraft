Meet The Team

Passionate high schoolers interested in engineering, dedicated to inspiring the next generation

UpDraft Engineering is powered by a diverse team of engineers, educators, and volunteers who believe in making engineering accessible to all. Our team brings decades of combined experience in education, technology, and community building.

Sonu Rao

President & Founder

Sonu Rao is a junior at Lake Travis High with a passion for computer science, engineering, and business. She loves to challenge herself with hard classes, participate in many competitions, and host classes (like UpDraft)! When she's not busy researching Brain Computer Interfaces or learning about Quantum Entanglement and engineering innovations at MIT, she draws, swims, plays the piano, and volunteers with the American Red Cross. Sonu hopes to serve her community and spread her love for engineering, showing its potential and amazing uses while mentoring the next generation of future leaders, innovators, and creators.

Elora Sekour

Chief Operating Officer

Elora Sekour is a rising junior and is excited to be a part of the UpDraft team. She is a proficient coder with experience with Java, python, and C#. Her hobbies include Model UN, choir, and art. She hopes to someday become a product manager, leading teachnical and logistical team operations. She is ready and willing to help anyone start on their path to a career in STEM.
